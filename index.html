Beat Saber Teased 3 NEW songs for their 8th anniversary

This teaser is incredibly exciting for the Beat Saber community, especially for those who love high-energy, complex electronic music. This release lands on May 28th as part of Beat Games' 8th Anniversary celebrations, where they traditionally drop highly anticipated free tracks to thank the community.

Here is an expanded breakdown and clarification of what you can expect from these three powerhouse artist pairings:

1. Skybreak & Daeya

  • The Vibe: Melodic Dubstep / Color Bass / Cinematic EDM.

  • What to Expect: Skybreak is famous for his incredibly bright, lush, and intricately sound-designed "color bass" (a vibrant, melodic evolution of dubstep). He recently made waves in the game with "Accelerate" (with Teminite) on the Monstercat Mixtape 2. Partnering with Daeya—who is known for her ethereal, soaring, and emotional vocals—means we are almost certainly getting a massive, beautifully melodic bass anthem.

  • Gameplay Prediction: Expect sweeping Arcs that follow emotional vocal leads, contrasted against fast, bursts of rhythmic streams during the drop.

2. Boom Kitty x MDK

  • The Vibe: Glitch Hop / Electro House / Chiptune Heavy Bass.

  • What to Expect: This is a dream collaboration for rhythm game fans. Both artists are legends in the Geometry Dash and broader rhythm gaming ecosystem.

    • Boom Kitty brings heavy, bouncy, and aggressive bass lines (think "Burning Sands" or "Peeled").

    • MDK (Morgan David King) is famous for finger-breaking, hyper-energetic, video-game-inspired chiptune electro (like "Fingerbang").

  • Gameplay Prediction: This track will likely be an absolute chaotic masterpiece. Expect highly rhythmic, syncopated mapping, tricky crossovers, and a relentless BPM that will put your endurance and wrist speed to the test.

3. Zakka G

  • The Vibe: Hardcore / Speedcore / J-Core / Artcore.

  • What to Expect: Zakka G is a staple producer in the arcade rhythm game scene (featured heavily in games like Arcaea, Chunithm, and Muse Dash). They specialize in high-BPM, breakcore-infused electronic music that features beautiful, intricate piano melodies layered over devastatingly fast drum beats.

  • Gameplay Prediction: If you are a fan of the Camellia tracks in Beat Saber, this is likely where your true Expert+ challenge will hide. Zakka G’s signature speed means this map will heavily favor complex Chains, rapid stream patterns, and frantic note densities.

💡 Important Platform Reminder: Keep in mind that Beat Games officially ended content updates and support for the PlayStation 4 and PlayStation 5 (PSVR/PSVR2) versions. This means the May 28th anniversary drop will be exclusive to Meta Quest and PCVR (SteamVR) players.

Get your sabers charged—between the rhythmic intensity of Boom Kitty x MDK and the sheer speed of Zakka G, May 28th is going to be a workout! Which of these three tracks are you most excited to clear first?
